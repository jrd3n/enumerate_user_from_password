Chocolate Covered Ramen Noodles

Ingredients

  • Dried ramen noodles - 1 pack
  • Chocolate - 100 grams
  • Peanut butter - 2 tablespoons

Instructions

1. Melt the chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl.
2. Stir in the peanut butter until smooth.
3. Break the dried ramen noodles into smaller pieces and mix them into the chocolate and peanut butter mixture until fully coated.
4. Lay the chocolate-covered noodles on a parchment paper and allow them to cool until the chocolate has hardened.
5. Enjoy your chocolate-covered ramen treat!
